THE CHARACTER

,,Safety never takes a holiday.''

Paul Blart is a mild-mannered man who worked as a security guard in a New Jersey mall. For years, he has applied to become a cop, but he always fails the physical exam because he has hypogmycemia and tends to pass out when his blood sugar is low. 

After the shocking events from the year 2009 / 2015, he has been contacted by the secret underground crime syndicate known as Crime.Net

Paul knew this was a life seeking opportunity so he agreed to join the infamous crime network and become a part of the PAYDAY gang.
